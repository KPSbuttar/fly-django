Introducing Fly

FLY (Financial Literacy & Entrepreneurship for Youth) is a free app available on Android, iOS, and the web. Built from a grassroots, non-profit perspective, and backed by Libro Credit Union, as well as financial professionals, FLY was built to help you learn more about managing and achieving financial goals.

Fully Responsive

You can get FLY in the Apple App Store or Google Play Store, but you can also use it via the web, and it will look great in any modern web browser

Cloud-Based

Want to check your goals from multiple devices? No worries, FLY keeps your goals in the cloud where they are sade, secure, and accessible.

Built For Youth

FLY works on all major platforms, and was built just for you! We want it to be fun and engaging, so don't be afraid to tell us how we can improve!

Features Of FLY App

Open Source
